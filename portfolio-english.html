Featured Work

COLORSCODEX is a book designed in blog format (I started it in October 2016); it is made up of 49 pages (divided into two languages, Spanish and English), with more than one hundred recipes (in each language) and illustrated with photographs taken with the camera of an iPad iOS 9.3.5.

Colorscodex is a photographic guide of dishes made only with vegetables and the style has an unmistakable seal: Mediterranean Savor.

☰

Unlike the blog, where I used different templates, provided by the Google Blogger server, and I modified the color and the font of the text to create a very personal style, now I write the code to create the structure, give color and design to my first page.

It is the Final Project, corresponding to the first phase of the Udacity Front-End Nanodegree Course (January 2018).
